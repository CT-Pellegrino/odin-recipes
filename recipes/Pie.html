Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is our famous italian home made lasagna recipe. It is simple yet robust enough for any occasion, whether special or holiday. Join us to learn how to make this quick dish and feed up to 20 people!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Sauce
  • Lasagna rolls
  • Patience

Steps

  1. Ground cheese and Beef together
  2. Prepare Sauce
  3. Bind Ingredients Together
  4. Bake at 445F for 25 minutes
  5. Enjoy with loved one!
